# What is Statistics?
=> Statistics is a branch of applied mathematics that involves the collection, description, analysis, and inference of conclusions from quantitative data.
1. Descriptive Statistics is a deals with summarizing and describing the main features of a dataset
2. Inferential Statistics is a involves making inferences, predictions, or generalizations about a large population based on data collected from a sample of that population
